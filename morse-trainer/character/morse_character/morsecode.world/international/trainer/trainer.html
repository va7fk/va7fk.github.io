Change log

    Keyboard Shortcuts

    The following keyboard shortcuts may be used:

    On the numeric keypad:

    • Previous / next session: 2 & 8
    • Previous / next segment: 4 & 6
    • Play / pause: 0
    • Replay current segment from start: 1 or Enter
    • Play next (random) segment: 3
    • Hide text toggle: 5

    On the main keyboard:

    • Previous / next session: < & > or p & n
    • Previous / next segment: , & . or j & k
    • Play / pause: /
    • Replay current segment from start: r
    • Play next (random) segment: m
    • Hide text toggle: h
    • Flashcard toggle: c
    • Play first segment of current session: R
    • Stop: s
    • Loop (repeat) toggle: l
    • Shuffle toggle: f
    • Download: d
