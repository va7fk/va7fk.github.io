VA7FK Station Equipment

Over the years various equipment has come and gone through the VA7FK hobo shack. What I've learned is everything affects antennas, I prefer knobs and buttons to touch screens and menus, orange back-lit LCD displays are easiest on the eyes, and I don't care for much of the mainstream gear designed post-2010. I've all but written off the idea of further using amateur radio gear with color touch screens unless things greatly improve in terms of UI, pixel density, and color scheme options. Having an on-board band scope is nice but it's not worth the compromises and ugliness.
